About Us

The Dream 02 was established on 6th June, 2021 with an purpose to help everyone from ranging young athletes to dedicated veterans to achieve their dreams.

We are committed to

  • Purpose,
  • Impossible to I'm possible,
  • Vision and Mission.


Vision

The Dream 02 has a vision of growth in physical, social and educational aspects not just in sports but also as the best version of the athlete in their daily lives

Mission

To reach out to as many who are aspirants to train for the dream and make all things possible by the way and methodology of our training
